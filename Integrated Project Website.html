English Version

The development of technology has brought many significant changes in our life. Some of the developments appeared to be quite useful, but due to their overuse, they resulted in polluting our dear planet. Much of the recent developments aim at reducing pollution and making Mother Earth green and beautiful once again! Solar energy is one such development. In our daily life, we use exhaustible resources much faster than they are renewed. Solar energy can help us reduce the amount of fossil fuels and other precious resources we use on a day-to-day basis. We wanted to try to heal nature with one small step at a time. So, we thought of an idea to charge our phones with the sun’s energy. We all use power banks to charge our phones in our daily lives but almost none of us are aware about how dangerous power banks are and how much harm they cause to the environment. Their radiations harm the ozone layer! If they malfunction, they also release harmful gases that harm plants and animals. One phone uses over 7 kilowatts to charge itself per year! When we charge our phones, we are hurting the environment every time! You must be thinking that, now we cannot even charge our phones, which are an essential part of our life, without hurting Mother Nature? Fear not! We have solved that problem. We have come up with an eco-friendly way to charge phones- a Solar Powered Mobile Charger! What better way to make our surroundings clean and green than to use the energy of the great sun! This wonderful invention can charge your phone whenever placed in sunlight. Isn’t it just amazing? Our group, consisting of Aadya Nanda, Astitva Bala, Rajvi Malik, Shaurya Aditya Jauhar, Shiven Chadha and Tashvi Negi, has worked together to present you with an affordable Solar powered mobile charger!
